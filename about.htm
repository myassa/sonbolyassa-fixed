The Artist

Sonbol Yassa is a portrait artist residing in Brampton, Ontario, Canada.

He started his painting career at an early age, completing many portraits and religious paintings while he was still a student in his native Egypt.

After immigrating to Canada, he has concentrated on developing his talent and studying technique with portrait classes at a number of colleges.

He combines his career as a Professional Engineer with his passion for painting to produce portraits in oil that are stunning in their simplicity and likeness to the subject while at the same time uncover the subject's personality.

Sonbol has completed a number of commissioned works and is accepting new commissions.
